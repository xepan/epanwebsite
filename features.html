Product Introduction

Discover more about Epan.

  • Epan Services, a state of the art ERP made specifically to solve management problems in your business. Epan gives you a helping hand in managing all the activities of your business with transparency and accountability. It won’t be wrong to call it a virtual manager.

    Epan services is a application based product where you get separate applications to manage different sections of business like marketing, commerce (e-commerce as well as traditional commerce) to manage every aspect of your business (quotation, order, invoice, shipping, payment collection, follow up, accounts and balance sheet), etc.

Security And Privacy

Security And Privacy Policy.

  • Your data is secure with us.
    -> It won’t lost. We are having high-available 3 layer clusters of data and storage. As the bottom, you always have facility to backup and restore your data in easy manner.
    -> We will keep it private.
    -> Only with your permission we will use it for internal purpose. Only for understanding slowness of system if found, to resolve any bug you register on support system or for system performance optimisation.
    -> Your data will always be your property and will not get shared in any manner.
    -> With International standards, our staff has no or restricted access to databases, that too for only maintenance purposes.
    -> We provide database clustering in case server goes down, that comes only with cloud based purchases.
