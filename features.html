Product Introduction


Epan Services, a state of the art ERP made specifically to solve management problems in your business. Epan gives you a helping hand in managing all the activities of your business with transparency and accountability. It won’t be wrong to call it a virtual manager.

Epan services is a application based product where you get separate applications to manage different sections of business like marketing, commerce (e-commerce as well as traditional commerce) to manage every aspect of your business (quotation, order, invoice, shipping, payment collection, follow up, accounts and balance sheet), etc.

You think of a business problem and the right solution is present in epan services.

Epan erp is basically collection of modules which manage different aspects of your business.

Project And Task management is the right epan application for that. With extended reminders get notifications on Emails, SMS or Desktop pop ups. Just assign task to employee and set reminders.

See if you are getting proper result from your sales team on your investment with real time analytics. Do better planning and know what’s going best from your sales team and plan accordingly. Boost your sales management instantly.
  • Accounts: Manage your accounting and that also in the easiest and simplest way. You don’t need to be an accounting expert to handle accounts when you use epan services. A very unique and innovative approach to handle accounts by any non-accounts person and yet get your reports ready for your accountant.
  • Commerce: From Lead to Sales and Quotation to Invoice everything is very well managed in Epan services. Epan is customizable, effective and single application can manage online and your offline sales both. Yes, E-Commerce and Traditional business sales both from single software. Design your discount vouchers, Taxes as per govt. Policies, define custom shipping rates.
  • Store and warehouse: multi-warehouse management, item wise stock management. Get reports based on Item Custom fields (like colors, size etc too). You just manage your purchase and sales. Define multiple email accounts, define which email is to be used for mass mailing, send email history to your customers in just one click. Marketing staff can always see history to deal with any client in case of staff change. No lead loss now. Or no need to ask ‘what's going on with Mr.x’ to your team. Just click and read.
  • CMS: With epan, creating and managing website is as easy as eating a cake. Just drag and drop elements to make a professional looking website. We at Xavoc are creating huge template base for you to select and proceed.

Installations


ERP As A Service (Cloud Setup)

That’s Quick, safe and recommended setup. Each Cloud setup have their own isolated database. Getting state of the art security from international standard cloud server, Our applications and privacy policy is strong.

Pros: Quick Setup, Easy and Automatic Updates to bugs and new features.

Cons: always required Internet availability. Yearly fees.

On-Premise

Setting up Epan service at your own premises.

Pros: Can work on Intranet, local and physical approach to your database and system.
Cons: Heavy Investment in server setup, and High-Speed internet in case of E-Commerce and Communication system. No Easy and free Updates. High maintenance cost in Internet and server. High initial ERP purchase cost. In case of faults costs of service is required. Highly qualified server and database administrator
