Documentation - Developers

xEpan is extensible, in every aspect. Its a complete platform to extend and develop almost any application in super rapid fashion. If you wish to use your own application in ERP or you want to customize any portion by hook your code in, its just possible. Epan is hosted service for xEpan, but it keeps your database totally isolated in separate databases, so you need not worry about disturbing any other client by mistake.

This section defined how you can develop your own xEpan Applications.

xEpan is build on Agiletoolkit framework.
