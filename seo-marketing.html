Search Engine Optimization for Epan

Epan, straight out of the box, comes ready to embrace search engines. Its features and functions guide a search engine through the posts, pages, and categories to help the search engine crawl your site and gather the information it needs to include your site within its database.

Epan comes with several built in search optimization tools, including the ability to use .htaccess to create apparently static URLs called permalinks, blog rolling, and pinging. There are also a number of third party plugins and hacks which can be used for search engine optimization (SEO).
